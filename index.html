What's this?

A café heat map. During my travels I noticed a correlation between the density of cafés and how lively the neighborhood is. Also, it's usually where the hipsters are.

This is an experiment to aid me finding nice areas in cities I travel. Maybe I bump into my next favorite place with the help of this once?

It works with OpenStreetMap data, uses the Overpass API and it's not accurate but it gets the job done.
