What's this?

A Hipster district finder. During my travels I noticed a correlation between the density of cafés and how lively the neighborhood is.

This little map is an experiment to aid me finding nice areas where I am more likely to bump into my next favorite place.

It works with OpenStreetMap data, uses the Overpass API and it's not accurate. It gets the job done though.

Legend

Other things might as well be interesting, so I created multiple layers

  • Brown-yellow areas are cafés
  • Reds are universities
  • Blues are libraries
