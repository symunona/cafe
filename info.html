What's this?

Every city has nice and less nice parts. During my travels I noticed a correlation between the density of cafés and how much I like the area.

To test my hypothesis - and find nomad-friendly areas in new cities - I put together this little map to guide me to areas where I am more likely to bump into my next favorite place.

... and I am also just into maps.

Legend

Other things might as well be interesting, so I created multiple layers

  • Brown-yellow areas are cafés
  • Reds are universities
  • Blues are libraries
